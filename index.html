How it works

Two equal pillars, one mission

Real‑time deck monitoring pairs with AIS analytics for complete coverage.

On‑deck AI object detection

Detects shark finning on boat decks using existing cameras and edge processing.

Computer vision runs on edge devices or IP cameras to continuously monitor decks. When risk is detected, the system emits an event and optional clip for immediate review — not a full video stream.

Designed for privacy and bandwidth efficiency: analysis happens locally, and only structured alerts are sent to shore.

Accelerated AIS intelligence

Fast analysis highlights patterns associated with IUU fishing for rapid response.

We enrich AIS with ML to flag suspicious behavior such as loitering, dark activity, and transshipment. Vessels receive a rolling risk score and trend indicators.

Insights are available via a simple console or API, and can trigger alerts for your ops stack.
