AI object detection

Detects shark finning on boat decks or boats using existing cameras.

Our Object Detection Models run on your cameras or drones to continuously monitor boats. When risk is detected, the system emits an event and clip for immediate review

Designed for efficiency and speed: analysis happens locally, and only structured alerts are sent to you.

Accelerated AIS intelligence

Analysis patterns associated with IUU fishing for increased efficiency.

We enrich AIS with ML to flag suspicious behavior such as loitering, dark activity, and transshipment. Vessels receive a rolling risk score and trend indicators.

Insights are available to user immediately.
